Seminars

Every year I do a series of seminars for the wonderful folk at Computer Science House to get the new members introduced to new tools, and to help those already proficient become monsters with their toolchain of choice.

I keep these seminars up for my own convenience and so that others can customize them. If you notice any errata, feel free to open an issue on Github I'll be happy to make the fix. Eventually I'll get all my slides to the same web-based presentation format. Unfortunately, there's a seemingly limitless supply of them, meaning I switch constantly while looking for one that stands head and shoulders above the rest.
